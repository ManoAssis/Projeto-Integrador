About Us

We work with confectionery for over 3 years in the city of Campinas, offering

products for parties and birthdays, weddings and baby showers, serving

also corporate events.

We currently serve in Campinas and São Paulo, also working in partnership with

event decorators, always creating our own decorations for the

products with great delicacy and creativity.





Story

The taste for confectionery sharpened the curiosity to test ingredients and create new ones

recipes for many years. According to the search for gourmet brigadeiros and cakes

increased, we developed our creativity and technique through courses, always

adapting and reinventing our recipes in an intuitive way.

Choosing high quality ingredients and taking care of hygiene standards

and food safety ensure product quality and a smile on your face

of those who experience.




We have partnerships with companies for special events, such as Christmas parties

birthday, mother's day, father's day, baby showers, halloween, wedding

and many others!

Our partners offer the best products for each production step,

projecting our customers' dreams.




Our mission is simple: bring more joy

and unique flavors for our customers.

Made with love has more flavor
